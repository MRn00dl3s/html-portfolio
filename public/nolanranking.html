The Best Christopher Nolan Movies

My top 5 Nolan movies.

Tenet

Very underrated, I love the execution of the film and the concepts it presents.

Intersteller

Incredible visuals, insane detail, really cool premise.

The Prestige

Amazing plot twist and gets better every rewatch

Inception

Really good visuals and most action packed Nolan original (not Dark Knight)

Oppenheimer

In IMAX probably the best movie experience I've ever had. Long drama but interesting and intense
